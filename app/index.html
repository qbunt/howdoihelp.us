We've noticed that everyone is feeling a cocktail of emotions due to last week's election results, but the common theme is that everyone wants to help and many don't know how. On the flip side of that, there are many projects bubbling up that help but not everyone knows about them. That's why we are creating a help hub. howdoihelp.us is a website that aggregates and organizes all of the projects that people are coming up with to help.

We are currently working on the site and kindly as you to check back soon when we are live. Thank you!
